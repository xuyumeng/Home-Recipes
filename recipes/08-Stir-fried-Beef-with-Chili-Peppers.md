## Ingredients
- Beef
- Chili peppers (e.g., bird’s eye chili)
- A small amount of ginger
- Garlic
- Fresh coriander


## Instructions

1. Slice the beef thinly **against the grain**, then roughly chop if needed.  
   Add a small amount of light soy sauce, oyster sauce, white pepper, and starch. Mix well, then add a little cooking oil and mix again to coat.

2. Heat a wok with oil, add the beef, and quickly stir-fry until separated.  
   Pour a splash of cooking wine along the edge of the wok. Remove and set aside.

3. Add some oil to the wok, then stir-fry the minced ginger, garlic, and chili until fragrant and spicy.  
   Add soy sauce, stir-fry until aromatic.  
   Return the beef and stir-fry over high heat briefly.  

4. Add chopped fresh coriander, toss quickly, then season with a little salt. Mix well and serve.
