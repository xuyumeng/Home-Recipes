### Ingredients
- Chicken wings
- Ginger (sliced)
- Green onion
- Cooking wine
- Light soy sauce
- Dark soy sauce (optional)
- Coca-Cola
- Salt

---

## Instructions

### 1. Blanch the Chicken Wings
- Clean the chicken wings and score both sides.
- Place in a pot with cold water, add ginger, green onion, and a little cooking wine.
- Bring to a boil, skim off foam, and cook for 2 minutes.
- Remove and drain well.

### 2. Sear and Simmer
- Heat oil in a pan and add the chicken wings.
- Pan-fry until both sides are golden.
- Add ginger slices, light soy sauce, and dark soy sauce; stir to coat.
- Pour in enough Coca-Cola to cover the wings.
- Cover and simmer over medium-low heat for about 15 minutes.

### 3. Reduce the Sauce
- Add salt to taste.
- Turn to high heat and reduce the sauce, stirring until it thickens and coats the wings.

---

## Notes
- The Coca-Cola adds sweetness and helps create a glossy, rich sauce.
- Great served with rice—the sauce is especially flavorful!
